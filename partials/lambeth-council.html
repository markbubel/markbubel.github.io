Project: Lambeth Council (London, UK) Description: Designing data visualization software to support council initiatives. Lambeth Council in London needed a new way to support making decisions based on collected council data. They did not have data visualization software in use, so we started from scratch. My role: brainstorming, sketching, prototyping, usability testing with council members Notes: This was an group project for my Design Experience 2 class, so the download is a presentation on which our team worked on together. However, the prototyping and testing phases feature much of my work. Download the design presentation (pdf, 6.6mb)
