I'm a human centered researcher and designer.

I am a user experience designer who understands the theory behind everything that I create. I like to take a human-centered approach to all design: user research, prototyping, testing, and iteration.

I believe that technology fundamentally improves lives, and I’m passionate about designing for good. You'll see this belief reflected in my work.

I'm a graduate of University College London where I completed the MSc in Human Computer Interaction with Ergonomics. My class modules have prepared me work in design, research, and ergonomist roles. Before I joined UCL, I worked for 5 years in the student loan servicing industry in the US where I contributed to and improved the UX of my company’s products and services.
