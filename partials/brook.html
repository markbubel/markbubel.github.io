Project

Brook (https://www.brook.org.uk/)

Description

Brook was aware that their website services needed to be redesigned from past research. Each student chose a part of Brook's website to identify strengths, weaknesses and new user requirements to improve their services.

My role

Heuristic evaluation, card sorting, grounded theory analysis, interviews, report writing for development team

Notes

This was an individual project for my Understanding Usability and Use class.
