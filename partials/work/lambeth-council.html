Lambeth Council (London, UK)

Lambeth Council in London needed a new way to support making decisions based on collected council data. They did not have data visualization software in use, so we started from scratch.

This was an group project for my Design Experience class, so the presentation was a team effort. However, the prototyping and testing sections feature much of my own work.

My role

  • brainstorming and desk research
  • sketching
  • prototyping
  • usability testing with council members
