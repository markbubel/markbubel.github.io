Lambeth Council (London, UK)

Project goal

London's Lambeth Council needed a better way to support making decisions based on collected council data.

Project outcome

A prototype information dashboard that allows the user to view past council performance, focus on council goal process, zoom to a specific level of detail, and supports navigational preference.

My role

  1. Brainstorming and desk research
  2. Iterative sketching
  3. Prototyping with UXPin and Invision App
  4. Usability testing with council members

This was a collaborative project with others on the team, so while I had a part in every step of the UCD process, much of my work was focused on the sketching and prototyping phases.
