About Me

I work across the entire product design process

I’m comfortable leading and working through the entire product design process, including research to find product opportunities and understand needs to designing concepts, prototypes, and production-ready interfaces.

But my enjoyment comes from designing

My core, technical interests and skills as a designer are focused on designing products that feel considered with a high level of interaction and visual craft.

I have some technical skills, too

I have some formal, academic training in computer science, and I was a front-end developer during my first job. This gives me an edge over other designers as I work well with (and understand) engineers, QA, and scrum masters.

I want to have a positive impact

I’m most interested in how design contributes to better healthcare for all. I care—deeply—about my responsibility to design products that create better digital health experiences—for everyone. You'll see this belief throughout my work.
